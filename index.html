Sedentary Ping Pong

All the fun of Ping Pong without having to leave your chair!

How to Play

Ping Pong will count up to your number from 1 with the following exceptions:

  • Any numbers divisible by 3 will "Ping"
  • Any numbers divisible by 5 will "Pong"
  • Any numbers divisible by 15 will "Ping Pong"
