Sedentary Ping Pong

Have all the fun of Ping Pong without leaving your chair!

How to Play

Sedentary Ping Pong will generate a list from #1 to the number you enter while converting...

    Any numbers divisible by 3 to "Ping"

    Any numbers divisible by 5 to "Pong"

    Any numbers divisible by 15 to "Ping Pong"
